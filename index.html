History should not be a school subject

27 April 2024

History is all about looking back to the past, learning from the past and apply what is learnt to the current, present world.

However, the world is developing at such a pace that the world in the past is vastly different from what we are now. In History, students study wars, two different factions sending thousands of men into the battle fields. But with the invention of nuclear bombs, there is now rarely any physical sword clashing, but cold wars where both parties try to out-scare their opponents with more powerful bombs. The world is changing rapidly, and straying further and further away from the past.

History is also taking time away from studying other subjects, like languages, Mathematics, and the sciences. Instead of learning about rules and policies in the past that change over time, we can instead learn about timeless, universal laws like Newton's 3 laws of mation. History, while not completely useless, is taking time away from learning knowlegde that can be more easily applied to our lives, and therefore should be removed.
